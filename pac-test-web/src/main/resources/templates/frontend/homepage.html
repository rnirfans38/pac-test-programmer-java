Instructions for use

If you don't have an ATM account, you must register first in the sidebar on your left by pressing the register button.

Customers who have registered and have an account can check the latest balance information. By clicking the balance information icon in the sidebar.

Transaction menu, where customers want to withdraw cash, deposit cash or transfer. By clicking the transaction icon in the sidebar

Balance History, is a history of every transaction carried out by each customer. If customers want to see, they can click on the balance history icon in the sidebar.
